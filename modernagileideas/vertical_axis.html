Vertical Make People Awesome & Make Safety a Prerequisite

The vertical aspect of the Modern Agile badge represents primarily the human element of the product we are trying to produce.
The Although all the values work together the vertical values are designed to focus on the humanity of building a product.
Make People Awesome is the first of the vertical values.
This is a broad value that requires you to think who am I trying to make awesome and what can I do to make them awesome.
The people to be made awesome are everyone involved in the software including customers, stakeholders, and the team.
  • Awesomeness means different things to different people in the process.
  • Customers are made awesome whenever you provide the ability to do something they were unable to do before or do it in a better way.
  • Stakeholders become awesome when they get the value they are looking for in their domain of work.
  • Teams are made awesome when they enjoy their work and feel a sense of accomplishment.
Make safety a Prerequisite is the second of the vertical values.
Who needs to be safe? Everyone.
  • Safety is a Prerequisite when team members are allowed to make mistakes and correct them.
  • Safety is a Prerequisite when customers can rely on the code to not do bad things to them.
  • Safety is a Prerequisite when stakeholders know they will be protected from losing value.
Making safety a Prerequisite can mean all of these things and more. It involves.
  • Quality
  • Trust
  • Freedom
  • Honesty
  • Diligence
